Risk Assessment

High Priority Risks
Transportation Disruptions
High Risk

Ferry cancellations or delays between islands and mainland, potentially affecting international flight connection.

Mitigation: Book morning ferries, allow full buffer day between Koh Phangan departure and Malaysia flight, research alternative routes
Heat-Related Health Issues
High Risk

Dehydration, heat exhaustion, sunburn from high temperatures (28-35°C) with high humidity.

Mitigation: Plan activities during cooler hours, maintain hydration (2-3L daily), wear sun protection, carry electrolyte supplements
Accommodation Availability
High Risk

Limited availability in Koh Phangan during high season.

Mitigation: Book Koh Phangan accommodation immediately, have backup options in different areas, consider higher budget if needed
Visa/Immigration Issues
High Risk

Problems during Thailand exit or re-entry.

Mitigation: Carry all previous visa documentation, have proof of onward travel, maintain copies of all immigration documents
Medium Priority Risks
Digital Work Disruptions
Medium Risk

Unreliable internet, particularly on Koh Phangan.

Mitigation: Research backup work locations with reliable WiFi, download essential files for offline access, use mobile hotspot as backup
Health Access Limitations
Medium Risk

Limited medical services in island locations.

Mitigation: Carry comprehensive medical kit, know location of best medical facilities, ensure travel insurance covers evacuation
Dengue Risk
Medium Risk

Mosquito-borne illness more common in coastal Thailand.

Mitigation: Use high-DEET repellent consistently, wear long sleeves/pants during peak mosquito hours, choose accommodations with screens/nets
Location-Specific Risks
Koh Phangan
Location Risks

Road Safety: Motorbike rental requires extreme caution - consider alternatives

Water Safety: Check conditions before swimming, particularly during low season

Beach Security: Don't leave valuables unattended, even on quieter beaches

Krabi
Location Risks

Boat Operators: Use only licensed operators for any excursions

Wildlife: Be aware of monkeys at viewpoints - secure food and belongings

Tides: Check tide schedules for beach visits - some areas have strong currents

Kuala Lumpur
Location Risks

Flash Flooding: Heavy afternoon downpours can cause urban flooding

Air Quality: Occasional haze events possible (check daily AQI)

Transportation: Use official taxi services or ride-hailing apps rather than unofficial taxis
